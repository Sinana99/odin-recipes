Potato Salad recipe

Description

Here we will show you how to cook this delicious low carb Japanese potato salad/p>

Ingredients

  • crab
  • salad
  • potatoes
  • cream
  • corn kernel

Steps

  1. chop the vegetables
  2. cook the potatoes on a pan with small amount of fat
  3. chop the potatoes
  4. mix the salad and potato with the cream
  5. season with a little amount of salt and vegetable oil
